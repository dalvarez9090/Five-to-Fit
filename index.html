EVALUATION AND PLANNING

Start your path to true and lasting results by knowing where you are and deciding where to go. Five to Fit started as a research idea where our coaches designed, executed, and presented measuring methodologies to clearly monitor the physical development of athletes. Although the methodologies were used in groups, we were able to evaluate and analyze each individual to improve their athletic performance. Based on these experiences, it is very important to understand the value of having a baseline and a starting point. You need to start with a personalized evaluation and obtain a baseline of your performance. We will design for you the very best program based on your initial conditioning and your goals. Besides, we are project managers ready and capable to integrate milestones and feedback loops to your training program, with monitoring tools to make your experience diverse, dynamic and forward-moving.

DETOX PROGRAM

Clean your mind through your body and refresh your spirit by getting rid of the toxins and waste your body accumulates. We come into contact with harmful toxins throughout our daily routines. Depending on your lifestyle, you will have different levels of toxicity in your body: those drinks you had on the weekend; that burger you had for lunch today; the pollution on your city, etc. Detoxification is a must as it enhances your organs' functions, balances your hormones, and optimizes your entire body- efficiency. Five to Fit will renew your body constantly and fine-tune your machinery for the physical challenges ahead.

OPTIMAL NUTRITION

Visualize your body as the most advanced piece of technology you will ever meet. More than fuel, your body requires OPTIMAL NUTRITION. A computer, a car, a cellphone or a plane do not only need a battery but they need an enclosed environment in which multiple complex processes can perform automatically and without issues. OPTIMAL NUTRITION accomplishes the same for your body. Five to Fit will make sure your body achieves the transformation it intends to go through, by advising on the right amount of nutrients your body needs and by fitting your nutrition habits into your daily schedule. Your body will be very thankful and it will show it to you by making you healthier, happier, stronger, and increasing your overall well being. The program follows a scientific approach of macro regulation and calorie intake monitoring customized to your specific nutritional plan.
